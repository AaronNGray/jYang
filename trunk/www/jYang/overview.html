jYang is written with

  1. -java 1.5

  2. -javaCC


This version follows the draft-ietf-netmod-yang-05 document.


Errors in YANG speciﬁcations are printed on the standard error output. jYang stops checking at the ﬁrst lexical or syntaxical error but try to check more than one semantical error.


Modules at http://www.netconfcentral.com/modulelist are checked (but see at toDO list)
